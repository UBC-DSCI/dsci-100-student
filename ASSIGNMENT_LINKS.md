# Worksheet and Tutorial Assignment Links

This document contains links that will automatically download the week's worksheet and tutorial into your student JupyterHub account. If you don't have a student JupyterHub account yet, follow the instructions below to create an account with your **cwl@student.ubc.ca email account**.

# How to Register for DSCI 100 JupyterHub

1. Open a browser (Chrome preferred) and go to https://dsci-100-student.stat.ubc.ca

2. On the first page click the small red house that has "Log in" text

3. On the next page, click the highlighted text for 'Sign up' in 'Sign up to create a new user'

4. For the email address **only enter your cwl@student.ubc.ca email address**. Choose an easy to remember username and password and keep a record of it as you'll be using it for the rest of the term. Then complete the captcha and click 'Create User'.

5. You should receive a successful prompt that tells you a confirmation email was sent to your **cwl@student.ubc.ca** email address.

6. Close the browser tab and follow the instructions [from this link](https://ubc.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0016900) to log in to your student email account. 

7. In your email account there should be a confirmation email titled 'Action required: DSCI 100 JupyterHub account approval", if you don't see it, check your trash and spam folders.

8. Click the link in the email above to finish the registration process. Then go back to https://dsci-100-student.stat.ubc.ca and log in with the username and password you created earlier in step 4.

9. Click an assignment link below to open a worksheet/tutorial.

If you need to change your password, log in and use the link here https://dsci-100-student.stat.ubc.ca/jupyter/hub/change-password. If you forgot your password, please contact your instructor for a password reset. Include your username and student email address.
